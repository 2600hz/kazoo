*** Monolithic
#+BEGIN_CENTER
All components are interconnected
#+END_CENTER

*** Monolithic
#+BEGIN_CENTER
[[file:./images/kazoo_stack_monolithic.png]]
#+END_CENTER

*** Monolithic
 - Pros
   - Maximum redundancy
   - Maximum utilization
 - Cons
   - No isolation from DoS and other attacks
   - No regions
   - Easy to have a cascading failure

*** Geographic Zones
#+BEGIN_CENTER
Components connected in a single region, regions interconnected
#+END_CENTER

*** Geographic Zones
#+BEGIN_CENTER
[[file:./images/kazoo_stack_geographic.png]]
#+END_CENTER

*** Geographic Zones
 - Pros
   - Isolation from DoS and other attacks
   - Isolation from Overloaded Servers
   -Better media path (possibly)
 - Cons
   - Under utilizes resources if poorly planned
   - More management
   - More equipment

*** Private Service Zones
#+BEGIN_CENTER
Service offerings are isolated, DB is still connected
#+END_CENTER

*** Private Service Zones
#+BEGIN_CENTER
[[file:./images/kazoo_stack_service_zones.png]]
#+END_CENTER

*** Private Service Zones
 - Pros
   - Moderate Isolation in Key Risk Areas
   - Easier to sell as a private service
   - Easier to deal with spikes in volume/load
 - Cons
   - More Expensive
   - Requires More Management
   - Data Integrity Issues
