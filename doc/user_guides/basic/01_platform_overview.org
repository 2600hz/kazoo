** Reality sinks in

*** Option 1: Keep going as-is                                      :B_block:
    :PROPERTIES:
    :BEAMER_env: block
    :END:
 - Keep building everything in-house
 - Hire staff to support Operations
 - Hire programmers

Pro:
 - Unique
 - Fully customizable
Con:
 - Responsible for everything
 - Unproven
 - Learn “as you go”

** Reality sinks in

*** Option 2: Buy a Switch                                          :B_block:
    :PROPERTIES:
    :BEAMER_env: block
    :END:

 - Buy a carrier-grade platform
 - Move all your customers over
 - Hire minimal staff to run Operations

Pro:
 - Proven and polished
 - Second line support
Con:
 - High upfront costs
 - Difficult to know if the product will sell
 - Unknown customization abilities
 - No differentiator between competitors

** Reality sinks in

*** Option 3: Go with Hosted                                        :B_block:
    :PROPERTIES:
    :BEAMER_env: block
    :END:

 - “Rent” a carrier-grade platform
 - Move all your customers over
 - Use their cost model and telcos

Pro:
 - Proven and polished
 - No huge up-front cost
Con:
 - Difficult to know if the product will sell
 - Little customization abilities
 - Inability to bring own carrier
 - No differentiator between competitors
